Frequently Asked Questions

Are you open to research collaborations?

Absolutely! I'm always excited to collaborate on projects related to quantum systems, condensed matter physics, and computational modeling. Feel free to reach out with your ideas.

Can I use your simulation code for my research?

Most of my code is open-source and available on GitHub. Please check the individual repository licenses. If you use my code in published work, I'd appreciate a citation or acknowledgment.

Do you offer tutoring or mentoring?

I occasionally mentor undergraduate students interested in computational physics and quantum mechanics. Send me a message describing your background and goals.

What is your current research focus?

I'm currently focused on quantum many-body systems, particularly Bose-Einstein condensation in optical systems and topological phases in 2D materials. I'm also preparing for graduate studies in condensed matter physics.
