Investigated the low-energy excitation spectrum of photon BECs in dye-filled microcavities. Derived hydrodynamic equations and predicted novel collective modes arising from photon-photon interactions mediated by the dye medium.

A Spectral Method for Solving the Gross-Pitaevskii Equation in Anisotropic Traps

Developed and benchmarked a highly efficient spectral algorithm for solving the time-dependent GPE. Demonstrated superior accuracy and convergence compared to standard finite-difference methods for anisotropic trapping potentials.
