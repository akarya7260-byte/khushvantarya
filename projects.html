How I Work

My systematic approach to research and computational physics projects.

1. Problem Formulation

Define the physical problem, identify relevant theoretical frameworks, and establish clear research questions and hypotheses.

2. Literature Review

Survey existing research to understand current state-of-the-art, identify gaps, and build upon established methodologies.

3. Model Development

Construct theoretical models and derive governing equations. Design computational algorithms for numerical solution.

4. Simulation & Analysis

Implement code, run simulations, validate results against analytical limits and benchmark data. Perform statistical analysis.

5. Interpretation

Extract physical insights from numerical results. Connect findings to broader theoretical frameworks and experimental data.

6. Documentation

Write clear technical reports and papers. Document code thoroughly. Prepare presentations and visualizations for communication.
